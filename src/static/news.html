Journal Publication — EURASIP

Open-access article in the EURASIP Journal on Audio, Speech, and Music Processing (Springer). Presents Hi-Audio as an open-source web-based platform for collaborative multitrack music recording, annotation, and sharing for scientific research.

Hi-Audio received a Best Poster Award (short paper) at the 9th Web Audio Conference, IRCAM, Paris, November 19–21, 2025. Paper: "A Maximum Length Sequence-based method for robust round-trip latency estimation in online Digital Audio Workstations."

User Evaluation with La Scène

Preliminary user evaluation study with 22 participants, organized in collaboration with La Scène, the music association of Télécom Paris. Results cover task success rate, usability (SUS), and perceived workload across recording, annotation, and collaboration features.

Onsite Music Workshop — Télécom Paris

29th of April 2025 at Télécom Paris. Hands-on workshop for musicians of all levels to experience collaborative DAW recording. Participants contributed to a collective rendition of Au clair de la lune — one of the oldest known recorded songs.
